## Common objections — how Demi should respond

**"Solar is too expensive."**
A 10 kW system costs PKR 1,575,000. Average grid bill for that load is PKR 35,000–50,000/month. System pays itself back in 3–4 years and produces free electricity for the next 21+ years. We also offer 3-year bank financing where the EMI is often less than the current bill.

**"What if the panels break?"**
25-year manufacturer performance warranty on Tier-1 panels. We've been installing since 2018 and our claim rate is under 0.5%. If a panel underperforms, the brand replaces it free.

**"Will it work in load-shedding?"**
On-grid systems shut off when WAPDA goes off (safety requirement). For backup during load-shedding you need a hybrid system with battery — see hybrid pricing above. A 10 kWh battery handles a typical 3–4 hour outage on standard household load.

**"Can it run my AC?"**
Yes. A 1.5-ton inverter AC draws ~1.2 kW. A 5 kW system runs 2 ACs comfortably during the day. For night-time AC usage on battery, you need at least 15 kWh battery backup.

**"How long does it last?"**
Panels: 25+ years (with 80%+ output guaranteed at year 25).
Inverter: 10–15 years, often replaced once over the system's life.
Battery: 8–10 years for lithium, then replacement.

**"Do you do post-installation service?"**
Yes. Free first maintenance at month 6. Optional annual maintenance contracts after that. Emergency WhatsApp support 24/7. Most issues resolved remotely via the monitoring app.

**"What's the best brand?"**
For panels: Jinko Tiger Neo or Longi Hi-MO are our top recommendations — best price-to-performance.
For inverters: Huawei SUN2000 is premium, Solis and Growatt are excellent value.
For batteries: Pylontech for budget, BYD or Huawei LUNA for premium.

**"Will it work on a tin roof / shed?"**
Yes, with the right mounting frame. Add ~PKR 25,000 per kW for tin roof reinforcement and angled frame.

**"What about cloudy days / winter?"**
Output drops 30–40% in December–January. Annual production accounts for this. A properly sized system still covers your full year's electricity bill.

**"Tax / customs / import?"**
All our pricing is post-tax (17% GST included). No hidden customs charges — we handle import for our material.

## Lead qualification — what Demi should capture

For every inbound call, capture:

1. **Name** of the caller and decision-maker (if different)
2. **City / area** of the property
3. **Property type** — house, shop, factory, farm, plot
4. **Roof type** — concrete, tin, RCC, plot (ground-mount)
5. **Average monthly electricity bill** (PKR) — this drives system size
6. **DISCO** (LESCO, K-Electric, IESCO, etc.)
7. **Goal** — bill reduction, backup, full off-grid, net-metering income
8. **Battery interest** — yes/no
9. **Timeline** — immediate, 1 month, 3 months, just researching
10. **Budget range** if they share it
11. **Best callback time**
12. **WhatsApp number** for sending the proposal

## When to book a site survey vs. send a quote

- **Bill < PKR 30,000/month** → send a 5 kW or 7 kW indicative quote on WhatsApp first; site survey only if they're serious.
- **Bill PKR 30,000 – 100,000** → book a free site survey within their city.
- **Bill > PKR 100,000** → book a senior engineer site visit, prepare a custom commercial proposal.
- **Factory / large commercial** → always book an in-person engineering survey before quoting.
